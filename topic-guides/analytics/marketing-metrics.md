### Engagement Metrics
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Engagement rate
- Time on site
- Pages per session

### Conversion Metrics

- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Lead quality score
- Sales qualified leads (SQL)

### Revenue Metrics
- Customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
- Marketing ROI
- Revenue attribution
